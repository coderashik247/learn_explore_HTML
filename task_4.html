Bhuna Khichuri

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 1 cup moong dal (yellow lentils)
  • 2 sliced onions
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt, turmeric, and garam masala

Cooking Steps

  1. Dry roast the lentils until lightly golden.
  2. Wash and drain the rice and dal.
  3. Sauté onions in oil until golden.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and spices, and cook well.
  5. Add rice and lentils, mix thoroughly, add water, and cook until done.

Ilish Bhapa

Ingredients

  • 6 pieces of hilsa fish
  • Mustard paste
  • Mustard oil
  • Green chilies
  • Salt and turmeric

Cooking Steps

  1. Marinate fish with salt and turmeric.
  2. Make a paste with mustard, green chilies, and mustard oil.
  3. Coat the fish with the mixture.
  4. Steam for 20–25 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with steamed rice.

Begun Bharta

Ingredients

  • 1 large eggplant
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1 chopped onion
  • Mustard oil
  • Salt

Cooking Steps

  1. Roast the eggplant until soft and peel off the skin.
  2. Mash it with green chilies and chopped onion.
  3. Add mustard oil and salt, mix thoroughly.
  4. Serve with hot rice.
